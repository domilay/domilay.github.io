About Me

I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the Data Science Institute, University of Technology Sydney, where I also obtained my Ph.D. in Computer Science. My research focuses on graph data mining, time-series forecasting, and machine learning for complex large-scale systems, with a strong emphasis on applying AI to real-world critical infrastructure challenges.

During my doctoral studies, I published 10+ research papers in CORE A*/A conferences and JCR Q1 journals. In parallel, I have collaborated with major Australian infrastructure operators — including Sydney Trains and Sydney Water — on AI solutions for transport, water, and urban systems, several of which have been recognised as finalists in national Australian research and innovation awards.

I am committed to building trustworthy, operationally deployable AI systems that strengthen critical infrastructure resilience.
